Ideal city

When we were young we grew up with fairy tales about a wonderland with beautiful castles, nature and magic. We read stories about "The most beautiful fish in the sea" where everyone was tolerant of each other, about worlds on clouds where everything is soft and safe. Through these fairy tales, we increasingly formed an image about an ideal world. As we grew up, these dreams were shattered multiple times. Still, we want to achieve our realistic dream city as best as we can and we incorporate these fairy tale values into it. Imagine, you are walking on the soft green grass and feel the warm sun on your skin. All around you are children playing and people laughing on benches. A little further on, you can hear some birds chirping in the deciduous trees and life is good. The inhabitants are tolerant of each other and there is a safe, cozy atmosphere. In this way we see that this dream city can also be realized in real life. In the video below, you can see these ideas brought to live.
